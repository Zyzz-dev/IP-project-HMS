About us

We, at Rajalakshmi Engineering College (REC), provide a wide choice in selecting the type or rooms in a hostel to suit every budget, with an aim of providing a decent stay and the best possible learning environment. The quality of life is one of the most important factors considered by the students deciding to stay in the College hostels. The stay at our campus provides a wealth of experience by developing qualities such as camaraderie and social responsibility. Life on campus helps the students not only to study but also to socialize with the peers. Friendships formed on the campus last a lifetime. REC provides a comfortable and safe housing to all its students. The students housed in the campus are encouraged to use the gymnasium and all indoor gaming facilities.


Hostels

Boys Hostel - I Years

This hostel is for the male students belonging to UG I Year. These consist of only 4 Sharing Non-AC Bedrooms. The estimated fee is 6500 rupees.

Boys Hostel - II, III, IV Years

This hostel is for the male students belonging to UG and PG II, III, IV Year. These consist of only 4 Sharing Non-AC Bedrooms. The estimated fee is 6500 rupees.

NRI Boys Hostel - I, II, III, IV Years

This hostel is for the male students belonging to UG and PG I, II, III, IV Year. These consist of Single Sharing AC Bedrooms and 4 Sharing AC Bedrooms. The estimated fee is 7000 rupees.

Girls Hostel - I, II, III, IV Years

This hostel is for the female students belonging to UG and PG I, II, III, IV Year. These consist of Single Sharing AC/Non-AC Bedrooms and 4 Sharing AC/Non-AC Bedrooms. The estimated fee is 6500 rupees for Non-AC and 7000 for AC.
